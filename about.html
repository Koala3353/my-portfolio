My Story

Hi, I'm Keene Brigado (Koala)

Ever since I was in elementary school, I've been passionate about programming and robotics. When the COVID-19 pandemic disrupted my robotics dream in grade 7, I turned obstacles into opportunities. I dove into online programming courses, mastered Java, and began creating Discord bots. By 13, I ventured into freelancing, and over two years, I earned more than $2,000 on increasingly complex projects.

Competitions Background

In 2023, I returned to school and trained for a local robotics competition, but due to health issues, I had to drop out. Undeterred, I joined the 13th FIRST LEGO League Philippines in 2024, where my team won first place in the robot game category. This achievement earned us a spot at the FLL Open European Championships in Norway, where I doubled my efforts and led the team to a seventh-place finish out of fifty teams in the robot game category. This journey taught me invaluable lessons about resilience, dedication, and balancing ambition with humility, shaping me into who I am today.
